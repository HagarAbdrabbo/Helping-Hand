Regular events

Ladies Night

In this event, Helping Hand invited only women for an evening, in favor of the girls in Dheisheh refugee camp in Bethlehem.

These evenings offered music, dancing, delicious food and competitions, where you had the opportunity to win great prizes while supporting a good cause!

Football Tournament

HelpingHand held a soccer tournament in December 2017, the purpose of which was to raise money to start a girls' school in Dheisheh refugee camp in Bethlehem, Palestine.

At present,our focus area is how 'sport and movement' can create well-being and friendships among the children whose everyday life is filled with chaos and insecurity.

The girls in the refugee camps are often overlooked because it is the boys who are the most outgoing in their reaction to the conditions they live under. There is because most of the projects are focusing on boys.

We believe that the girls need the same opportunities to strengthen them in the same conditions they live under.
