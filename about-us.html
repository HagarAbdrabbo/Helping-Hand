Our story

The idea of ​​HelpingHand started in October 2012, when the association's founder Huda Chaaban was in Palestine. During the trip, Huda noticed how the Palestinian children life. They were working on the street instead of going to school, wearing dirty and worn clothes and didn’t have enough to eat and drink.

Huda realized the privileges she had in her childhood in Denmark. She had a safe and secure childhood with her parents who give her love and care.

Huda initiated this charity to help these chi- ldren access the most basic needs such as clean water, food, schooling, warm clothing, and a roof overhead. The idea behind is to collect 10 kr/m. from many peoples. We want to clarify that even small amounts can make a big difference if we are just enough .
